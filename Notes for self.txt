font-weight controls the thickness of the letters
- low number = thinner (100)
- big number = thicker (700 is default bold)
- can write as number or words

grid-template-columns: repeat(4, 1fr);
- Creates 4 equal-width columns in the grid
- Each column takes up 1 fraction (1fr) of the available space
- | card | card | card | card |
